# loc-portfolio

Who Am I?

Professional Presentation

I am a French native translator with over ten years of experience specializing in English-to-French localization and translation. Throughout my career, I have collaborated with several leading localization agencies on projects for major international companies such as Apple, Dell, Microsoft, and CrowdStrike.

My work has involved a wide range of content, including software user interfaces, online help systems, user guides, marketing materials, websites, and legal documents such as contracts, terms and conditions, and privacy policies. These projects have allowed me to develop strong expertise in both technical and marketing localization, while gaining a deep understanding of how language, technology, and user experience interact in global products.

I regularly work with industry-standard CAT tools such as Trados Studio 2022, Phrase, Smartling, and XTM, which allow me to ensure terminology consistency, maintain translation quality, and integrate seamlessly into modern localization workflows.

Over time, my interest has increasingly shifted toward the technical side of localization. Working closely with translation platforms, file formats, and localization pipelines has inspired me to expand my expertise beyond translation itself. My goal is to become a localization engineer, combining my linguistic background with technical skills to help design, manage, and optimize localization processes.

To achieve this, I am currently developing technical competencies in tools and technologies commonly used in localization engineering, including:

Programming and scripting: Python, JavaScript, and basic Bash scripting

Version control: Git and GitHub

Localization automation and CLI tools: Okapi Framework, Translate Toolkit

File formats and markup languages: XML, HTML, JSON, YAML, and XLIFF

APIs and integrations: working with localization platform APIs

Automation and workflow management: CI/CD basics and localization pipeline automation

Quality assurance tools: automated localization QA and validation tools

By combining my translation experience with these technical skills, I aim to bridge the gap between linguistic expertise and engineering workflows. My objective is to contribute to building efficient localization pipelines that help global products reach international audiences quickly, accurately, and naturally.
